# **App Name**: SunGuard

## Core Features:

- Wearable Device Integration: Connects to popular fitness trackers (e.g., Apple Watch, Fitbit) to continuously monitor key vitals like body temperature and heart rate.
- Intelligent Risk Prediction: An AI tool that processes wearable data and environmental input (e.g., humidity, heat index) to provide real-time, personalized sunstroke risk assessment.
- Emergency Contact Configuration: Allows users to securely add and manage up to three emergency contacts (phone numbers, email addresses).
- Automated SOS Alerts: Triggers instant SMS/email alerts to designated emergency contacts upon detection of critical health thresholds or high-risk conditions identified by the AI tool.
- Customizable Alert Thresholds: Enables users to set personal health parameters and preferred notification sensitivity for early warnings.
- Live Vitals Dashboard: A clear user interface displaying current body temperature, heart rate, activity level, and the system's calculated risk status.
- Preventative & First-Aid Guidance: Offers immediate, actionable advice for prevention and first-aid steps if a high-risk state is detected, tailored by the AI tool based on the situation.

## Style Guidelines:

- The chosen light color scheme is anchored in the concepts of health, safety, and reliability. Primary color: A rich, dependable blue (#2966A3), chosen to evoke trust and a sense of calm. Background color: A very light, desaturated blue (#F0F2F4), providing a clean and clinical canvas that complements the primary hue. Accent color: A vibrant, clear cyan (#30E8E8) for highlighting alerts and crucial interactive elements, offering high contrast and immediate attention.
- The application will utilize 'Inter' (sans-serif) for all text elements. This font provides excellent readability, a neutral yet modern aesthetic, and objective clarity crucial for conveying important health information efficiently.
- Use clear, concise, and universally recognizable line-art icons. Prioritize icons that communicate health, alerts, safety, and biometric data effectively, ensuring high contrast against the chosen background.
- Employ a clean, card-based layout for the main dashboard to display real-time vital signs and risk assessment. Important alerts and emergency functions will be prominently featured with clear call-to-action elements, maintaining a user-friendly and uncluttered interface for urgent situations.
- Incorporate subtle and smooth transitions for data updates and navigation, minimizing distraction while providing clear feedback. Animated indicators for warning statuses will be simple and non-intrusive, drawing attention without causing sensory overload.
